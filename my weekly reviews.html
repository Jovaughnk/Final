Drake's Iceman Review

Baby Keem Casino

Baby Keem Drops his sophmore album Casino, the album has 11 songs which by the end of the album leaving you feeling you wish there were more songs on the album which i feel is a good thing. Casino is more of a introspective album about baby keems life before the fame and after. Songs such as No Security which is the first song off the album sets the tone of what the album is going to be about which is Keem talking about his life especially the times when he was younger and dealing with a mother who had a alcohol and drug addiction leading to her not being able to take care of him and leaving Keem with his grandmother. Baby Keem talks about his grandmother and living on the streets with his mother in other songs off Casino. Even though Baby keem has taken a more introspective route on Casino he still has song which catches your attention at the perfect time when you feel like it starts to become stale. An example of this is after the first track Keem comes with a catchy turn up song in the albums name but also a song Casino. These two songs i feel let you know what this album is going to be. My personal thoughts about Baby Keem before Casino came out was that he was a bit overhyped after releasing his first album I didn't really like how his voice sounded on medolic blue, but after listening to Casino I understand the hype. Hopefully Baby Keem is able to release new songs in the future to keep his momentum going.
